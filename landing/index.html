Rekalla helps older adults stay on top of their day — and gives the people who love them a simple way to help from anywhere.

Get early access See how it works

One glance says it all.

Three rings show the day at a glance — reminders done, routine followed, check-in complete. Big text, big buttons, plain words. No clutter.

The people who matter, one tap away.

The Memory Vault keeps family, doctors, medications, and important dates where they can always be found — with photos and one-tap calling.

  • Photos on every entry, so faces stay familiar
  • Call a person straight from their card
  • Simple search across everything

Family can help. From anywhere.

Caregivers connect with a simple 6-letter code — no shared passwords — then set up reminders, the daily routine, and the Memory Vault from their own phone.

  • Manage reminders, routine & vault remotely
  • See how the day is going
  • Connections can be ended at any time

A gentle rhythm for every day.

Morning, afternoon, evening — a simple routine to follow and check off, alongside reminders for medication, meals, appointments, and family calls.

  • Done and Snooze — nothing more complicated
  • A short daily wellness check-in
  • Everything sized for older eyes and hands
