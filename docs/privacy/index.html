Rekalla Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

Rekalla is a memory-support app that helps older adults and the people who care for them keep track of daily reminders, routines, important people, and wellness check-ins. This policy explains what information Rekalla stores and how it is used.

What we collect

  • Account details — your name, email address, and account type (Loved One or Caregiver), collected when you sign up.
  • Things you add to the app — reminders, routine steps, memory-bank entries (such as family members, doctors, and phone numbers), and wellness check-ins (mood, sleep, energy, notes).
  • Caregiver connections — when a caregiver connects to a Loved One's account using a connect code, we store that link so the caregiver can help manage reminders, routines, and the memory bank.

What we don't do

  • We do not sell your information to anyone.
  • We do not show ads or share your information with advertisers.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.

How your information is used

Your information is used for one purpose: to show it back to you and to the caregivers you have connected with, so the app can do its job. That's it.

Where it's stored

Your data is stored securely with Supabase, our database and authentication provider, and is protected so that each person can only see their own information — plus, for caregivers, the accounts they are actively connected to.

Who can see your information

Only you and the caregivers you have chosen to connect with. A connection can be ended at any time, which immediately removes the caregiver's access.

Deleting your account

You can permanently delete your account at any time from the Settings screen inside the app. This erases your account and everything in it — reminders, routines, memory-bank entries, wellness history, uploaded photos, and caregiver connections. Deletion is immediate and cannot be undone.

Not medical advice

Rekalla is a memory and coordination aid. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Children

Rekalla is intended for adults and is not directed at children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If we make meaningful changes to this policy, we will update this page and ask you to review the changes the next time you open the app.
